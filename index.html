Daniel Ahmed is an Algerian visual artist who blends surrealism and expressionism in a unique visual style. His works carry deep questions about identity, surveillance, and the human essence.

In his paintings, vortices, eyes, and geometric forms converge to create worlds that defy reality — inviting the viewer to contemplate what lies beyond the visible.
